Tuyin joined the Mathematical Sciences department in 2017. She is a mathematics teacher educator. Her research interests include: reasoning and proof, geometry for teachers, technology in mathematics education, teachers' content knowledge, hybrid teaching modalities, and equity. She is an MAA Project NExT Fellow and an AMTE STaR Fellow. She is also a co-director of the Georgia Southern University Robert Noyce Teacher Scholarship Program.
